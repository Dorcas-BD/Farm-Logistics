Some common questions
were often asked

Will I get my goods delivered in good conditions?

We have teams that makes research on farm produce and that helps us to provide the best truck or vehicle to move your goods.

Will I be able to track my goods?

Yes. Our system is integrated with softwares that will allow you to track your good at any time and also communicate with the driver.

What happens in case of emergency such as vehicle breakdown or accident?

All our vehicles and trucks get services before engaging in any delivery activites but if unfortunately there's a breakdown, we have backups. Your goods are insured in case of accident so your goods are safe.

Can I change my delivery location even after my goods are on its way?

Yes. We calculate our fee kilogram per kilometer so if your locationis changed the system will do the calcution for the updated service
